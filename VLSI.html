Hardware Neuron in Cadence and Neural Network in Python

Project Description

For my final project in ECE 4740: Digital VLSI Design, my partners and I designed and layed out a hardware neuron in Cadence Virtuoso. The neuron was designed to use 4 bit inputs and outputs, the new standard for AI inference. The neuron was implemented using a simple finite state machine, a 4-bit multiply-accumulate, and a ReLU activation function.
