Project Description

For the 2023 FRC Season, I served as Build Team Lead for FRC Team 639: Code Red Robotics. On top of being responsible for ensuring the completion of our robot within the 6-week timeline, I also designed two of our robot's subsystems: the end effector and the double jointed arms. The end effector was designed to pick and place cube and cone shaped game pieces, and the double jointed arms moved on a tilted elevator to move the end effector to where we needed it to be. I also largely wrote the below technical binder, which helped our team to win the 2023 New York Tech Valley Regional Innovation in Control Award.
